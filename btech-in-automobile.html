B.Tech Automobile Engineering

B.Tech Automobile Engineering has been an emerging course for young candidates who are having a liking towards cars or motorcars, their specifications, mechanisms, internal and external of a car. The cars or motorcars that we see now or probably even drive those are definitely an art of the multi- talented automobile engineers. It could be the designing, monitoring casualties including all the technicalities of a car. Developing new ideas for a change in the old concepts, creative mind-sets interested in automobile can take this up.

B.Tech Automobile engineering deals with the development of new concepts to replace the older concepts, manufacturing process, and formulating new models by designing and refurbishing the existing models with new technologies of automated cars. With the evolving era, we see a lot of commutation in the technology as well for a better modernized world. As the world is rationalizing so is the technology reaching the heights of success with creative and talented mind set coming up with remarkable ideas to shape the automobile world into a contemporary field of technology and ease for the community.

Eligibility criteria

  • The candidate must have completed their 10+2 from a recognized board or university.
  • The candidate must have completed their 12th with physics, chemistry and mathematics as mandatory subjects.
  • The candidate must have scored a minimum of 45% in PCM for General category
  • For those candidates who are from other categories such as SC/ST, PWD the minimum percentage to score is 40%.

Admission Process

The process of admissions for B.Tech Automobile Engineering is not very complicated. The admissions happen every year by conducting entrance exams and their 12th board results. Admissions happen either entrance exams or through direct admissions process.

  • KCET
  • JEE MAINS
  • JEE Advanced
  • COMED K

These are conducted on national and state level. They require a certain cut off percentage for affordable seats in colleges. There are some top universities privately funded which conduct their own entrance exams as the colleges are on demand and there is soaring competition.

They can either take up the university exam or go through national or state level entrance exam as both the scores can be viably considered. Some top university level exams are as follows:

  • PESSAT (PESSAT PES Scholastic Aptitude Test)
  • SRM JEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination)
  • Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT)
  • MET (Manipal Entrance Test)

There are certain states like Tamil Nadu, UP, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, and Delhi who take admissions through a centralized counseling process for which the students need to attend a national level exam like JEE Mains and Advanced.

Course Structure

Diploma in automobile engineering: Diploma in automobile engineering is a 3 year course that students can choose to take up after completion of their 10th standard for career opportunities in a shorter run.

B.E or B.Tech in Automobile Engineering: B.E or B.Tech in automobile engineering is a 4 year undergraduate course that students can opt after completion of their 12th standard. This course is a skill oriented multi- faceted with good career opportunities for students.

B.Tech in Automotive Design: B.Tech in Automotive Design is a 4 year undergraduate course which is a major specialization of automobile engineering which deals with the designing of various auto motor vehicles. Students can take this course after completion of their 12th standard.

M.E or M.Tech in Automobile engineering: M.E or M.Tech is a 2 year post graduation program that students can opt after completion of their graduation which is also considered as higher education and provides more knowledge onto this particular field besides this, also has a plethora of career opportunities.

  • Certificate course in Automobile Repairs and Driving.
  • Certificate course in Automobile engineering for a tenure of 1 year which can be as more beneficial on to knowledge.

Scope and Career

Engineering having a spectrum of professional courses, automobile engineering is one such undoubtedly pursued professions by the young candidates. With the day to day developments in the automobile industry it calls for more young and multi- faceted professionals in the field who are pretty zealous about their work. With the invention of automotive vehicles the future of automobile engineering has definitely made it more interesting for the aspirants who pull their skills and innovation into action to experiment their productivity.

The field offers exemplary career opportunities not just in India, but also abroad as the vision for this is globally enriched automotive vehicle for the development of the economy. The automobile market, both in India and abroad requires such individuals who can make a change in the technology in this area with an intuition of expanding by creating comforts for the people, and to work with the latest technology for stimulation in production and growth. Automobile engineers can work in different fields and can explore their talents. They can slog in production plants, service stations, transport corporations, insurance companies, motorsport companies and much more.
