Bachelor of Commerce Honors (B.Com Hons)

Commerce is regarded to be one of the most sought after stream following science is because it is all related to the study of economy, finance, accountancy, trading and its principles. Well, the most handpicked course in commerce is B.Com that every student would want to choose is because it aggregately covers the entire commerce field. But we also have different types of variations in B.Com too. Normally it’s a standard B.Com degree then it has B.Com Honors, B.Com Professional, B.Com Computer Science, B.Com LLB, etc.

B.Com is a 3 year undergraduate course which completely deals with the various aspects and foundation of trade and commerce principles with a much broader view towards the business. The meritorious students who want to pursue this degree in a specific specialization with an extensive and far reaching panorama towards commerce and its principles would usually prefer taking up B.com Honors. So this program offers the entitled candidates specialization in a wide frame of good managerial skills and besides this building proficiency in a particular area of organization or business.

B.Com honors offers candidates lucrative and highly payable jobs which will be an up elevation to their careers on which they will need to have good interpersonal skills and better scientific and technological knowledge in accordance with the pursued stream. In a world full of competency the work panorama has been viewed differently, thus here the technological and practical methods of handling an organization in terms of its rigid stability are a few aspects that a candidate in Bachelor of Commerce Honors learns.

B.Com honors nurtures the students with the skills, knowledge, aptitude and technicalities to meet the day to day challenges in business world. It gives a holistic approach towards the commerce panorama as it is a combination of accounting, economics, finance management, business and taxation etc. Also fosters the students to lead managerial positions with effectiveness and efficiency and maintaining good intellectual, social and interpersonal skills.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The candidates must have completed their 10+2 from a recognized board.
  • Candidates should have scored with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized board.
  • Most of the colleges accept applications on merit basis but few colleges have their own entrance exams and students need to clear that in order to avail admissions in the respective colleges.
  • The cut off percentage even in merit varies from college to college. Some top universities cut off can reach a loftiest 85% whereas some colleges can be a minimum of 60% as well.

Difference between B.Com and B.Com (Honors)

B.Com B.Com Honors
B.Com is a simplified undergraduate degree programme which covers the overall view of all the subjects taught in commerce. B.Com (Hons) is an undergraduate degree programme that is designed to inculcate business and key principles related to it in trade perspective.
Student cannot specialise in any particular or specific subject. In honors students have the option to specialise in a specific subject like accountancy, taxation etc.
The cut off percentage is relatively lower which is 45% minimum. The cut off is comparatively higher compared to b.com which is 85% minimum.
Career prospects and job profiles for both the degrees are same considering students with Honors are given more preference. Many companies and industries prefer BCom Honors graduates background due to their advanced training and expertise in one particular field.
It is a 3 year undergraduate programme. It is a 3 year undergraduate programme.
