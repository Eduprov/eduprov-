B.Tech Biotechnology Engineering

B.Tech Biotechnology Engineering is a very competent course in the engineering field. B.Tech Biotech is a very familiar course which has emerged in the field of science and technology. Biotechnology in engineering is a 4- year undergraduate program divided into 8 semesters which is amalgamating both life sciences, biological advancements in technology in order to study the micro-organisms or anti biotic to further utilize them for industrial process.

Biotechnology Is a major field which involves applied biology and scientific principles which involves the usage of advanced technology in the usage of living things in the field of engineering, medicine, technology and other sources. Biotechnology is a stream of which is a co-ordination of science and technology combining together to regulate new system in living organisms and other such things. B.Tech Biotech covers many topics such as micro biology, molecular biology, cell biotic, genetics and also technological subjects.

New technologies have been implemented in the industries which is now an emerging field of science and its principles for the advanced generation to effectively carry out those in many other industries. The most commonly used micro-biological techniques for DNA like PCR Polymerase Chain Reaction a technique used to make copies or replica of DNA, or molecular cloning to assemble DNA molecules or it could be MS Mass Spectrometry to sort ions based on their mass to charge ratio.

Eligibility criteria

  • The candidates must have completed their 10+2, with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics or Biology as their mandatory subjects from a recognized board
  • The candidate must have scored a minimum of 50% in PCM.
  • For the reserved categories such as OBC, PWD, and SC/ST we be with 5% relaxation.
  • Minimum age for admission is 17years.

Course Structure

Diploma in Biotechnology Engineering: Diploma in biotechnology engineering is for a tenure of 3 years and students can take up this course after completion of their 10th standard.

Undergraduate or degree program in B.Tech or B.E: B.Tech or B.E is a 4- year undergraduate degree course that students can take up after completion of 12th standard and is a very commonly opted course.

Post- graduation or M.E or M. Tech: M.E or MTech in biotechnology is the course that is usually considered as higher education and those interested in doing PG can do this after completion of their under- graduation program or B.Tech Biotechnology Engineering.
