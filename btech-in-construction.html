B.Tech Construction Engineering

B.Tech Construction Engineering is an integrated engineering discipline which formally operates and involves aspects of civil engineering as well as construction management. Construction Engineering mainly lays more focus on designing, managing and overseeing the projects in the construction industry. Construction engineering mainly manages and regulates construction projects and lays more emphasis on design of the structure to be built in accordance with all safety security measures and specifications. Construction engineering can be a major specialization in civil engineering.

The most chosen courses in the engineering spectrum, construction engineering is a 4 year under graduate degree in the engineering discipline which studies the design area and the development of a structure also involving principal essence of civil engineering. A construction engineer is mainly responsible to design, manage, oversee and build projects in a required industry. The projects might vary as they involve even the smallest of elements. The type of projects that they take up are drainage and sewage system, designing the water supply, developing roads, railways, highways or even constructing a building or a community. Construction engineering involves the engineers to take up various kinds of projects such as commercial building or civil building, electrical and sewage system, plumbing and heating system, ventilation in the building etc. They are also into major construction and development of governmental projects too. With a strong will power and determination altogether they put in the qualities will eventually lead them to a successful finishing of a project.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The candidates must have completed their 10+2 from a recognized board or university.
  • For admissions in diploma or UG course, the candidates must have passed their 12th with a minimum percentage of 60% in Chemistry, mathematics and physics.
  • For reserved categories such as OBC, PWD, SC/ST the minimum percentage would decrease around 5%.
  • After completion of 4 years degree students can get jobs.

Course Curriculum

Diploma in construction management: Diploma in construction management is for a tenure of 3 years and students can take up this course after completion of their 10th standard.

B.Sc in Construction Management: this course is a pure science course which is usually taken by students after 12th. This course is for a tenure of 3 years. And is more of a theory-based course.

Undergraduate or degree program in B.Tech or B.E: B.Tech or B.E is a 4- year undergraduate degree course that students can take up after completion of 12th standard and is a very commonly opted course.

Post- graduation or M.E or M.Tech: M.E or M.Tech in construction engineering is the course that is usually considered as higher education and those interested in doing PG can do this after completion of their under- graduation program or BTech construction Engineering.

Admission Process

Admissions for B.Tech Construction Engineering happens through entrance exams. Students can attend entrance exams Which are conducted on state level, national level and university level. Students need to be more precise on what level are they going to write and apply for the institution in prior. If they want to take up state wise entrance exams, they can go ahead with that. Students need to have all updates of entrance dates, registration and admit card issue. After the exam is being conducted and the shortlisted students will have to attend counselling round and personal interview round and are given admissions. Some institutions also provide management seats under the management quota. But the fees would be clearly very high. However, if the student is clear onto the institution and course can apply directly on the institutions portal and wait for document verification and clear the fee before the due date.

Scope and Career

Construction engineering being a specialized field of civil engineering definitely has rich scope and career prospects. Graduates from this field mainly find jobs from there relevant fields and specialization. This field is optimally present in almost every construction area. Mostly construction engineers are responsible for the work management and handling on the construction sites. The course is high on demand nowadays due to the increase in construction around and thus requires professional and skilled employees to fulfil the job requirements.

There are several jobs that involve management of construction, supervision, development and designing along with analysis of private as well as public sector projects which differ vastly such as dams, highways, canals, water bridges and sewage. The profession of construction engineers requires eminent skills and technical aspects to do the job.

Construction engineers can find jobs easily depending upon the level of skill and expertise that they have been groomed into. Students after the completion of their 4-years degree can either take up their future education such as MTech or M.E. in construction engineering and this helps them to have a better hold on activities. This field opens lucrative career opportunities for young and talented professionals and offers handsome salary packages which probes them to do the job with and excellence.
