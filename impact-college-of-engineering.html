Impact College of Engineering and Applied Science

Impact College Of Engineering And Applied Science is an autonomous institute. The impact group of institutions was established in 1980. It is a premier College of Engineering in Bangalore of the IMPACT Group of Institutions that has consistent record of excellence in technical Education. The college is located at Kodigehalli, Sahakaranagar, Bangalore, just 9 km from the Bangalore Cantonment Railway Station and 25 km from the Bangalore International Airport.

  • The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi
  • It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum
  • Department of Civil Engineering
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering
  • Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering
  • Department of Computer Science & Engineering
  • Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Debates
  • Public Speaking

The College has the separate hostel for boys and girls with all facilities.Sports- College conducts the various sport tournaments and provide all indoor and outdoor games facility. It has a jogging track and a modern gym.
